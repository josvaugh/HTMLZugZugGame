The Adventure of Zug Zug

Zug Zug the Orc with the Pet Rock named Noshia...
Yes he has a pet rock, he claims it giggles at him when he does immature acts and it rolls around on top of his fur pelts at night...
Some think the rock is crazier than him, but it's a matter of perspective right? It's harmless... or is it?
He's also known as the Ladies Man and most importantly the village weaponsmith!
He has his own shop and is in need of a quest to find more rare minerals in order to craft rare epic weapons!
Not only that, Zug Zug loves to collect weapons in order to train and fight with them since he is also a Master of Arm's, can't you tell by those muscles?
He isn't called the Ladies Man for nothing!
Now, onwards! Help Zug Zug find something rare and epic to craft his next weapon!
